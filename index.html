REFSA Living Wage Comparison

This tool allows you to compare your household income to the living wage in your state. Just enter your salary in the form (no data is stored anywhere, and all calculations happen in your browser), and the page will show how it relates to the living wage in your state. The map will show how your income compares to the living wage in all states.

The living wage per state has been calculated by REFSA based on the living wage calculated by the BNM, and modified using the spatial price index developed by the World Bank. The methodology and results are explained in this paper.

All calculations are done locally, so your data never leaves your computer and stays completely private.
